Recommended Workflow

Follow these steps for the best experience:

  1. Set Your Environment: Use the 'Screen Size' and 'Camera Intrinsics' controls to match your physical setup.
  2. Load Content: Click "Load Media Layers" to add and configure your scene.
  3. Calibrate 3D Scene: Click 'Set Scale', then measure a reference object in the scene (like a person) whose real-world size you know.
  4. Enter Real Size: When prompted, enter the object's size in meters to automatically set the parallax effect.
  5. Fine-Tune: Use the other sliders for artistic adjustments.
